Band Sunday - 18th December 2016

Celebrate the season with us this Sunday. A full brass band will be at the club house from 2:00pm until late. Please bring family and friends. Hot pot will be served along side drinks. Hope to see you there.

Annual Dinner Dance - 25th November 2016

Our annual dinner and dance will once agin be held at the Club House this year. The dress code is red coats and black ties. As usual visitors are welcome. Table sizes will be 10, 12 may be possible in certain circumstances. If you would like to book please use the contact number provided on the homepage. Invites must be sent in by the 11th of November.

Mens Open - 4th September 2016

As usual we are having our annual mens open. Please join us on the 04/09/2016. Ensure you have a group of four. Members and guests are allowed.

Ladies Team Open - 15th August 2016

We are once again hosting out annual ladies open. Please feel free to come as a player or spectator. All are welcome, tee off shall be at 8:00AM. For the sake of convienece please make sure that your team is ready to tee at your alloted time. Thank you and we hope to see you there.

Mens Team Open - 6th September 2015

Join us for our Mens open. The club is opening its doors to guests and members alike to join in this annual event. Ensure that you have a team already made and booked before Sunday 30th August. We hope to see you there.

Ladies Team Open - 16th August 2015

We hope you can join us for our open day. Tee off is at 8:00AM for the first team. Please make sure that you have informed us of your team before Sunday 9th August. Both members and guests are welcome. For any further details don't hesitate to contact. We hope to see you there.
