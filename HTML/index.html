Located in the grounds of the historic Royal Arley Hall, Wigan Golf Club offers an experience like no other. The 18 hole course leads its players through the senic woodlands of Haigh.

The club is situated just over 4 miles from the town center. Directions from the town center by car, will take you via Wigan Lane up to the second round-about and turn right onto Chorley Road, for a further 0.9 miles. Then take a right on the round-about onto School Lane, until you reach the traffic lights where you turn left onto Arley Lane, where visitors and members alike are met with a meandering drive as you go through the woodland and across the course, many have compared such a unique enterance to the famous Magnolia Lane in Georgia. Following the road to the club house. As you are aware the road does go through the course, so please be courtious to anyone who may be playing.
