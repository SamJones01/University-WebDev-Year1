A challenging start to your round, this hole is bordered by the Leeds & Liverpool canal to the right and trees to the left.

Hole 2

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Trees 410 4 4 401 392 4 2

A straight drive, avoiding the trees to the right, will put you in a good position to attack the pin with your second shot on this demanding par 4. However be aware of the two bunkers guarding the green to the left and right.

Hole 3

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Wall 369 4 14 357 336 4 8

The key to this hole is avoiding the trees to the left and right with your tee shot, as well as avoiding the bunkers and stream towards the end of the fairway that protect the green with your second.

Hole 4

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Mayor 140 3 18 134 129 3 18

Despite it being the lowest Stroke Index don't mistake this for an easy hole. If one does not account for wind on this hole then expect the ball to move towards the stream that runs to the left of the fairway or the out of bounds that are to the right.

Hole 5

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Hollow 361 4 2 349 329 4 6

Don't expect to be treated well on this hole if your drive isn't accurate. A testing second shot is hampered by a gradual inclining fairway, which can result in a blind shot to the green, combined with a sloping fairway which can pull your shot towards the dense line of trees on the right.

Hole 6

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Garden 308 4 16 300 290 4 12

A medium iron off the tee, avoiding the deep hollow to the left and the trees to the right, will put you in a great position to hit the green in two.

Hole 7

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Railway 462 5 12 454 440 5 4

A straight tee shot, avoiding the out of bounds of the canal to the left and the road to the right, can put you in with a good chance for getting to the green in 2.

Hole 8

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Pond 179 3 6 163 120 3 16

Beware the pond that runs along the left of the green on this challenging par 3 tee shot. The large slope on the green can make for a difficult 2 putt.

Hole 9

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Gap 364 4 8 321 305 4 10

An accurate tee shot avoiding the fairway bunker on the right will set you up to attack the green. Beware the shallow green can prove difficult to stop the ball on.

Hole 10

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
John Wild 520 5 9 499 451 5 3

A down hill dog leg right par 5, a good drive is essential if you are to go for this green with your second shot. Out of bounds running down the left of the fairway and around the back of the green along with a bunker to the right, offer the hole some protection. The green however is this holes main challenge. Do not be surprised to three or four putt here.

Hole 11

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Whelan 391 4 3 378 288 4 11

A left hand uphill dog leg requiring an accurate and long tee shot which will put you in a good position to attack the long green with your second shot. Protecting the entrance to the green are three deep bunkers to the left and heavy rough to the right.

Hole 12

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Pennine 181 3 11 162 150 3 17

A par three that appears to play longer than its yardage. Beware the hidden bunkers.

Hole 13

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Lakes 534 5 1 518 448 5 1

Unlucky for some, this signature hole with its stunning views is a true risk and reward. A long accurate tee shot, avoiding the out of bounds down the right and left of the fairway, leaves you to decide to either lay up with your second shot to the narrowest point of the fairway, or to attack the green from a distance..

Hole 14

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Mounds 336 4 13 332 309 4 5

Hitting the fairway with your tee shot is essential to come off here with a par or less.

Hole 15

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Ruin 154 3 17 141 127 3 15

Don't let this short par 3 deceive you. A hidden pond to the left is out of bounds with a green that is well guarded with four bunkers.

Hole 16

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Scott 350 4 7 327 309 4 13

A well positioned tee shot, avoiding the trees to the right and left of the fairway, on this dog leg right par 4 will allow you to attack the pin in two. Beware however the pond to the front right of the green.

Hole 17

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Road 323 4 15 315 306 4 9

Accuracy rather than distance is essential from the tee. Do not be long with your second shot as out of bounds is waiting just behind the green.

Hole 18

Name White Yards Par Stroke Index Yellow Yards Ladies Yards Par Stroke Index
Ravine 446 4 5 439 388 5 7

A truly strong par 4 to finish. A long and accurate drive, over a deep ravine, is essential to put you in a good position for your second. Anything right of the fairway will leave you blocked out from the green. Negotiating the large oak tree in the middle of the fairway with your second will be key to coming away with a par.
